Vegan Sesame Tahini Cookies recipe

SESAME TAHINI COOKIES

Makes roughly 8-12

INGREDIENTS:
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 tsp vanilla essence (I like a little more)
- 100g maple syrup
- 70g sesame seeds 
- 80g tahini paste
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 100g Ground almonds/almond flour

METHOD:
1. Preheat to 180Â°C. Line 2 baking trays with paper
2. In small bowL whisk almond flour with baKing powder
3. Roll dough into just smaller than golfball-sized balls. Or however big you want, whatever. 
4. Roll in sesame seeds
5. Flatten
6. Transfer to baking sheets

 Bake for about 8 minutes. Cool on a rack.
